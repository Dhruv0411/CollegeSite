Po's

elecommunication Engineering should - Attain a strong foundation in basic sciences, basic engineering courses in addition to core courses in Electronics and Telecommunication Engineering.
Be able to solve technological problems faced by industries in modern technological society as a prolific engineer.
Be able to adapt rapidly changing global industrial scenario due to advancements in technology/concepts and continue the process of lifelong learning to succeed in professional career.
Function as innovator and professional in product development.
Be able to function effectively as a member or team leader in core and multidisciplinary teams and attain project management competencies.
Be proactive to duties and responsibilities towards society and nation as an individual and engineer.

Peo's

To enable the students of this program to accomplish the PEOs, the curriculum is designed so that the graduates are able to - apply knowledge of basic sciences, mathematics and basic engineering courses as appropriate to the field of electronics and telecommunication engineering design and conduct experiments on electronics industrial set up, as well as analyze and interpret the resulting data. design a component, system or process to meet the specifications and requirements within pragmatic constraints. solve problems related to electronics engineering in interdisciplinary projects. solve industrial problems related to electronics, communication engineering, networking and maintenance of engineering systems employing electronic sub-system. understand and adapt universal skills and culture without losing human and ethical values. communicate (oral and written) effectively both individually and within multidisciplinary teams.understand and apply contextual knowledge to assess and solve social, health, safety, legal cultural and environmental issues related to engineering practices in general and electronics engineering practices in particular. recognize the need for and have the ability to engage in, perpetual learning by working on projects for which they have no prior experience andby adapting latest advancement in technology and concepts. interpret and update with contemporary issues affecting engineering industry. manage the project under execution effectively and professionally using the techniques, skills, and modern engineering tools necessary for engineering practice. assist in research and development activities. maintain quality, timeliness and continuous improvement.
